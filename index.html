About Me

My name is Jonathan M. Chauwa, I am a rising Junior at Berea College where I am studying Computer and Information Science with a concentration in Management Information Systems. I chose Berea because of the uniqueness of its liberal arts experience. It has not only helped to develop my technical skills but also the necessary soft skills such as the ability to work in a diverse team and to know when to lead and when to follow. This experience has been made possible through its labor program which require that students work a minimum of 10 hours a week in any of the schools departments.

I have worked as a student employee at my school's Information Systems and Services department, which serves all the technology needs of the school, for a year and half as a media technician. At the beginning of this summer, I took on a more technical position as a student software developer for the school. My roles now vary from debugging, designing and building web applications to conducting usability tests and managing correspondence with our customers.Additionally,I a member of the Student Judicial Board where I sit regularly to hear cases and recommend appropriate actions that encourage restorative justice.

In my free time, I like to enhance my technical skills by working on any interesting projects I find. I am passionate about change and how technology impacts daily business processes and decision making in industry. As a result, I am looking into a career that appreciates the applied aspects of technology and that is always breaking new grounds.
